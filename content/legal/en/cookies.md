# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** December 13, 2024

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas.

- **Session Cookies:** Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
- **Authentication Cookies:** Keep you logged in as you navigate the site

### Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website.

#### Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and usage patterns. These cookies collect information such as:

- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Browser and device information
- Referring websites
- User interactions

**Purpose:** To improve our website and understand user behavior  
**Data Retention:** Up to 26 months  
**Third Party:** Google LLC

#### Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity to understand user behavior through:

- Session recordings
- Heatmaps
- Click tracking
- Scroll depth analysis

**Purpose:** To improve user experience and identify usability issues  
**Data Retention:** Up to 1 year  
**Third Party:** Microsoft Corporation

### Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

- Language preferences
- Theme preferences (if applicable)
- User interface customizations

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

1. **Improve Performance:** Understand which pages are popular and identify technical issues
2. **Enhance User Experience:** Remember your preferences and settings
3. **Analyze Trends:** Understand how users navigate our site
4. **Optimize Content:** Determine which content is most valuable to our users
5. **Ensure Security:** Protect against fraudulent activity

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

**Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose your preferred cookie settings

**Firefox:**
1. Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Choose your preferred cookie settings

**Safari:**
1. Preferences → Privacy
2. Manage cookie settings

**Edge:**
1. Settings → Cookies and site permissions
2. Manage cookie settings

### Opt-Out Options

#### Google Analytics Opt-Out

Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on:  
[https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

#### Microsoft Clarity Opt-Out

Visit Microsoft's privacy settings:  
[https://account.microsoft.com/privacy](https://account.microsoft.com/privacy)

## Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies:

- Some features may not work properly
- We cannot remember your preferences
- Your user experience may be affected
- Analytics data will not be collected

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the website to function.
